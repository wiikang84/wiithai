## Product Direction
- wiiInfo is now being considered as a foreigner living-support app centered on:
  - nearby foreign/international grocery store discovery
  - store detail pages
  - saved stores
  - owner coupons
  - later legal remittance/mobile partnership referrals

- The strategic wedge is local offline store discovery in Ulsan/Gyeongju, not a broad foreigner community app.

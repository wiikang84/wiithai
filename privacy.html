Privacy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

What wiiInfo stores

If you sign in with Google, wiiInfo stores only the data needed to keep your saved items synced.

  • Google account ID, display name, email address, and Google profile photo URL
  • Saved phrase IDs
  • Selected source and learning languages
  • Created and updated timestamps

What stays on your device

If you do not sign in, saved items stay in this browser's local storage. They are not uploaded to wiiInfo.

Analytics

wiiInfo may use Firebase Analytics to understand basic usage such as language choices, category views, and feature use. It is used to improve the app, not to sell personal data.

Contact

For privacy, account data, or feedback questions, contact the app owner at wiikang@dyid.co.kr or use the wiiInfo feedback page.
